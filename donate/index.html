Here at VOMI, we hope to allow all migrants that have had to flee to Ireland a second chance of a brand new life, new job, family, education and more. But, with the funds that we are given by the government, our ambition to help every migrant we can isn't possible due to the lack of funding.

As of now, we have many migrants packed into tiny houses, but having almost ten people clustered into one small house or apartment with absolutely no privacy or any free time to themselves, and essentially living with strangers, living in fear for their kids and themselves.

We also hope to provide a third level education for migrants, but with no grants available for migrants, most of those who attend must depend on loans or self fund themselves for college.

This is where VOMI asks for your help to change these migrants lives for the better, with a voluntary donation you can make these migrants lives a better and safer one.

When clicking the donation button below, you're helping a migrant have a safe and comfortable home for their family, the chance for them to have a job opportunity and the chance for them to broaden their minds with a college education to have a job for life.

Every donation you give will help us in changing a migrants life, so thank you.

Who we are

VOMI is a group comprising of asylum seekers, refugees and migrants who come together to address their individual needs.

Our mission

Our purpose is to encourage asylum seekers, refugees and migrants (especially those who have been affected by long stay in Direct Provision) not to focus on their problems but to look at the positive side of life.
