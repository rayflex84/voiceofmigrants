ABOUT US

The group focuses on finding solutions to everyday challenges such as dependent living (Direct Provision System) and to address the stigmatization, prejudice and discrimination towards asylum seekers, refugees and migrants. Direct Provision system is a situation that slowly damages a personâ€™s life.

Our purpose is to encourage asylum seekers, refugees and migrants (especially those who have been affected by long stay in Direct Provision) not to focus on their problems but to look at the positive side of life.

We connect the newly arrivals with the local community and support them by providing a platform to learn or improve skills. We encourage integration, improving of skills, and most importantly motivate people to keep active for the benefit of their wellbeing.

Who we are

VOMI is a group comprising of asylum seekers, refugees and migrants who come together to address their individual needs.
