Who we are

VOMI is a group comprising of asylum seekers, refugees and migrants who come together to address their individual needs.

Our mission

Our purpose is to encourage asylum seekers, refugees and migrants (especially those who have been affected by long stay in Direct Provision) not to focus on their problems but to look at the positive side of life.
